Helpful ways to use this tool:

Quickly find generic alternative to medicines

When your doctor prescibes a medicine, quickly check on your phone for alternatives and ask whether the brand name product can be replaced with a generic.

1
2

Keep track of price changes for chronic medications

If you are on chronic medication you can periodically check to see whether the price of your medication has increased. Bookmark a page to be able to quickly refer to it later.

3

Quickly learn what price your medicines should cost

While waiting in the queue at the pharmacy, you can look up your medicine and find out how much it will cost you and what alternatives you can ask the pharmacist for.

?

Did you know? Medicine prices in South Africa are regulated and there are guidelines that ensure that you should never pay more than a certain amount for a specific medication. This price is determined using the single exit price, which is the maximum amount a medicine can be charged at.
